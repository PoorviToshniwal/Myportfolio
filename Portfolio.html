Newest Addition

Stackable @Global Game Jam

Stack, Share, and Balance your thoughts with “STACKABLE”! This game is all about building conversations—literally! It serves as a perfect ice breaker for house parties and small gatherings. Players stack speech bubbles, each shaped to represent emotions, on a wobbly, bubble-like base. Choose a topic, pick an emotion, and share your thoughts, experiences or stories before stacking your bubble. As higher the stack gets, the wobblier it becomes and the closer you are to the risk of it all falling down! It’s a fun, meaningful way to explore emotions, share stories, and strengthen connections—all while trying not to topple the stack!

Exploring Illustration & Narrative in Comics

This comic strip captures a playful and mischievous moment through expressive visual storytelling and dynamic narration. Inspired by classic comic techniques, the scene unfolds with lively dialogue, action-driven panels, and a clever twist at the end—turning the expected into the unexpected.The illustrations enhance the mood, using composition and character expressions to convey excitement, tension, and humor. Through this piece, I explored how pacing, panel transitions, and expressive gestures bring characters to life, turning a simple baking adventure into a humorous, cinematic moment.
